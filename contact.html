FAQ

Common questions.

Can schools request an EcoSense program?

Yes. Schools can contact EcoSense Africa to discuss eco clubs, climate lessons, recycling systems, or tree planting activities.

Does EcoSense Africa accept volunteers without technical experience?

Yes. Many roles focus on education, outreach, logistics, storytelling, and community support.

Can organizations partner on a project?

Yes. EcoSense Africa welcomes partnerships that align with environmental education, technology, clean energy, recycling, and restoration.

Are donations restricted to specific programs?

Donors can select a preferred purpose, or choose general support so funds can be directed to the most urgent program need.
