Our Purpose

Environmental intelligence for communities that need it most.

EcoSense Africa designs community-first environmental programs that combine sensors, youth education, recycling systems, clean energy, and restoration work.

Our organization is built around a simple belief: when communities can measure environmental change, understand it, and organize around it, they can protect the ecosystems that sustain everyday life.

Learn About EcoSense

Core Work Areas

  • Smart water monitoring for rivers, wells, and community sources.
  • Climate awareness programs for schools and local leaders.
  • Renewable energy pilots for villages and learning centers.
  • Recycling, tree planting, and clean air advocacy.
